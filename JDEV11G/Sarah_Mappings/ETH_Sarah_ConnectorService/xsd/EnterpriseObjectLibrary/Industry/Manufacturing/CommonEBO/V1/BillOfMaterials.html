BillOfMaterials

EBO Type Enterprise Business Object
CCTS Type
CCTS Dictionary Entry Name Bill Of Materials. Details
Description Bill of Materials (BOM) EBO is used for describing the composition of different types Bill of Materials such as Engineering BOM, Manufacturing BOM, Planning BOM etc. It provides a single level of item composition - thus, in a multi-level Bill of Materials, this EBO gives only the immediate children items but not the composition of the children items. If a child item is a sub-assembly, it will have its own BOM which will provide its composition.
