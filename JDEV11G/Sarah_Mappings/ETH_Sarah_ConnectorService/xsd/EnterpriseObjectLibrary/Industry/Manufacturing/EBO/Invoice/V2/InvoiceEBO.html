Invoice

EBO Type Enterprise Business Object
CCTS Type Aggregate Business Information Entity
CCTS Dictionary Entry Name Invoice. Details
Description Details about the invoice. The invoice represents a document that is used in two different contexts to achieve two different objectives. From a Receivables point of view, the invoice is a billing document that is sent to the customer or other inter-unit entities. From a Payables point of view, the invoice represents the document that comes into payable interface table/services from suppliers or other inter-unit entities.
